About Daily Wins

Daily Wins is a simple local-first progress tracker for daily checklist goals.

Data

Daily Wins stores your checklist, settings, and history on your device. Deleting the app or clearing app data may remove your history.

Common Questions

Do I need an account?

No. Daily Wins does not require an account.

Does Daily Wins sync across devices?

No. Daily Wins currently stores data only on the device where you use it.

Why do weights still add up to 100%?

Weights are relative. All visible items for the day count together as 100%.
