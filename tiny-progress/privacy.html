Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Daily Wins stores data on your device

Daily Wins is a simple progress tracker. Your checklist items, settings, and history are stored on the device you use the app on.

No account, ads, analytics, or tracking

Daily Wins does not require an account. The app does not include ads, analytics, third-party tracking, or social login.

No data is sold or shared

Daily Wins does not sell, rent, or share your personal information. The app does not send your checklist data to a server.

Deleting app data

Because your data is stored locally, deleting the app or clearing site data may remove your categories, checklist state, and history.

Future changes

If Daily Wins adds optional sync, accounts, analytics, or other online features in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are used.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed with Daily Wins in the app store.
